About AI for All '26

AI for All: Bridging Innovation, Talent & Opportunity in Nigeria is a community-driven event aimed at demystifying Artificial Intelligence and showcasing its potential to transform lives, careers, and industries across Nigeria. Hosted by Pie & AI Abuja, a deeplearning.ai community, this event brings together students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and AI enthusiasts to explore how innovation and collaboration can bridge the gap between local talent and global AI opportunities. Through interactive talks, hands-on sessions, and networking opportunities, participants will gain insights into the practical applications of AI in various sectors such as education, healthcare, business, and social impact. The event also seeks to inspire the next generation of AI leaders by connecting them with experts, tools, and platforms that can help them thrive in the AI revolution. Ultimately, AI for All is more than an event—it’s a movement to foster inclusive growth, empower emerging talent, and build a vibrant AI ecosystem across Nigeria.
